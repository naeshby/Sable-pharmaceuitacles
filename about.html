About Sable Medical

Our Mission

Sable Medical is guided by integrity, high ethical standards and respect for customers. We continually search for improvement, development and the most technologically advanced products to suit the need of our clients.

Our Vision

To be the first and last port of call for medical supplies and support services in Zimbabwe.

Our Corporate Goals

  • To build Sable Medical's success within Zimbabwe to help it launch it into the global markets.
  • To maintain and grow our market share and improve our profitability by focusing on product positioning and overall cost efficiency.
  • To strengthen our presence in existing customers by building on Sable Medical's comprehensive future product offerings and value added services.
  • To survive the future challenges we face and continue to grow our existing products market share by offering our customers outstanding products on time delivery and service.
  • To take corporate social responsibility seriously. Therefore, we actively support social and environmental initiatives that are aimed at improving the quality of life of the people of Zimbabwe and Africa as a whole.

Our Core Values

Pride in excellence and reputation
Commitment to customer service
Belief in corporate social responsibility
Commitment to health and safety awareness
Belief in opportunity based merit
Satisfaction and profit from work that benefit humanity
